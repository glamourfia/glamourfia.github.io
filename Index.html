Using Radio Frequency (RF), LED Light Therapy and Electrical Muscle Stimulation (EMS)

LED Light Therapy

LED light works by sending energy producing packets of light into deeper layers of the skin. LED treatments can minimize fine lines and wrinkles, treat sun damage and stretched marks. LED light provides a painless skin care treatment that plumps up skin by boosting collagen production and treat mild to moderate acne

Electrical Muscle Stimulation (EMS)

Helps activate the skin cell; stimulating rejuvination of collagen to improve skin elasticity, soften wrinkles, and leave the skin smooth and healthy

Radio Frequency (RF)

RF energy uses the tissue's natural resistance within various layers of the skin to transform the RF energy applied to the skin into thermal energy. It stimulates the dermis collagen regeneration thus increasing the thickness and density of the dermis. It will LIFT and FILL wrinkles, eliminate scars and restore skin elasticity and luster.
